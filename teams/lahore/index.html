Lahore Qalandars "Dama Dam Mast"

Lahore Qalandars (لاهور قلندرز‎) is a Pakistani professional cricket team representing the city of Lahore in Pakistan Super League. The team is owned by Qatar Lubricants Company, Ltd. and their home ground is Gaddafi Stadium
